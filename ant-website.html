Who We Are

Rooted in Community,
Driven by Transformation

ANT is a community-based organization working at the intersection of health, education, and economic empowerment in Eldoret, Kenya — and beyond.

Our Vision

Universal Access to Health & Opportunity

A world where all communities have access to quality healthcare and socioeconomic opportunities, enabling them to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Integrated Empowerment Programs

To empower communities through integrated health and socioeconomic development programs, fostering partnerships and promoting sustainable solutions that improve lives and transform societies.

Our Philosophy

The Wisdom of the Ant

Named after the industrious ant of Proverbs 6:6–8, ANT embodies diligence, foresight, unity, and collective effort — helping communities Acquire knowledge and Transform lives.

Building from Within

ANT's approach ensures that interventions are not only impactful but locally driven and maintained — creating community ownership that outlasts any single program.

What We Do

Our Programmatic
Focus Areas

ANT's integrated programs tackle health, livelihoods, education, WASH, and specific disease challenges — addressing root causes, not just symptoms.

Programmatic Focus

Increase Access to Healthcare

Expanding health services to underserved communities through mobile clinics, community health workers, and preventive care campaigns.

Learn more

Economic Empowerment

Support Livelihoods & Economic Growth

Equipping individuals with vocational skills, microfinance access, and entrepreneurial support to build sustainable income sources.

Learn more

Knowledge & Skills

Enhance Education & Skills

Delivering non-formal education, literacy programs, and skills training that empower youth and adults to participate fully in society.

Learn more

WASH

Improve Water, Sanitation & Hygiene

Constructing clean water points, sanitation facilities, and conducting hygiene behavior-change communication in schools and communities.

Learn more

Disease Management

Address Specific Health Challenges

Targeted programs on HIV/AIDS, TB, malaria, maternal health, and nutrition — reducing morbidity and improving community health outcomes.

Learn more

Strategic Goal

Foster Partnerships & Collaboration

ANT builds bridges with government agencies, faith-based institutions, NGOs, and private sector partners to amplify collective impact.
